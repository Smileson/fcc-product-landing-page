Belive in Technology, Make the change

The experience of a vinyl record player is unparalleled in music. It’s not just nostalgic by design, it’s immersive by its offering. Our turntables transport you to a time where music was the sole reason people gathered together. We encourage you to rediscover what it means to listen to an album, to be attuned and submerged in each note, each revolution, each song.

Our core features

Quality

At Victrola, expert engineers and artists come together to craft machines that deliver high-quality impact with a vintage feel. With the cleanest sound on the block and compatibility fit for the modern world, these beauties will last you a lifetime!

Integrity

We honor the creativity and passion behind your favorite albums. With authenticity and integrity in mind, our products will let you listen to music the way it was meant to be listened to.

Versatility

Never sacrifice compatibility and integration for style. With Victrola, you don’t have to choose. Our products can play almost every digital music format you can think of—plus, they’re Bluetooth compatible.

Style

Victrola turntables offer more than crisp, clean sound. They’re the centerpieces of any vinyl-lover’s living space. Sophisticated, sleek, and portable, these machines never go out of style.
